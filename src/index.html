Welcome to Our Market!

こんにちは、よこそ！(Hello, welcome!) The Mochi Market is an Asian-fusion bakery with locations in Utah and California. We are a small Asian-American owned business with a passion for baking and exploring different cuisines. Our goals for the Mochi Market are to spread diversity and provide people with a unique and delicious taste! We make pastries and desserts from various places across Asia, including Korea, Japan, India, and more!
