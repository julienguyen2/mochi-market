About Us

The Mochi Market was created by our three co-founders, Bahija, Bruce, and Vinh! From what originally started as a dream back in their college days, they have been working hard to run this Asian-fusion bakery as a small business.

Bahija Khadija

“My parents’ cooking back home has made me become passionate about food. My goal is to help others experience all of the delicious flavors just as much as I do.”

Bruce Gerard

“Working as a barista back in my college days meant working with a variety of different tastes and flavors unique for each customer."

Vinh Cahaya

“Growing up in an area where Asian representation in food is very limited, I want our business to inspire others of how unique and diverse food can be.”
